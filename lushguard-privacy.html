Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how LushGuard collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our Discord bot. By using LushGuard, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

LushGuard only collects and processes data necessary for its functionality. This includes: User IDs: For moderation logs and economy features. Server IDs & Settings: To store configuration preferences set by administrators. Economy & Leveling Data: To track and manage in-bot currencies and progression. LushGuard does not collect sensitive personal information such as email addresses, passwords, or private messages.

3. How We Use Your Data

LThe collected data is used strictly for the following purposes: Enabling bot commands and features. Maintaining server settings and preferences. Keeping economy and leveling statistics persistent. Ensuring moderation logs function correctly.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized services. We do not share or sell any user data to third parties. If LushGuard is removed from a server, stored data may be deleted within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Data Removal & User Rights

You have the right to request data removal. To do so, you can: Contact support through the LushGuard Support Server to request manual removal. Remove the bot from your server, which will trigger automatic data cleanup where applicable.

6. Third-Party Services

LushGuard interacts with Discord’s API, which follows Discord’s own Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for any data collection performed by Discord.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Users are responsible for reviewing changes periodically. Continued use of LushGuard indicates acceptance of any revisions.

8. Contact & Support

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please join the LushGuard Support Server.

By using LushGuard, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.
